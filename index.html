Our Mission

At the South Asian Canadian Film Federation, our mission is to elevate and empower South Asian voices within the Canadian film industry. We provide a vibrant platform for like-minded filmmakers with shared roots to connect, collaborate, and thrive.

Programs

From professional development to mentorship, workshops, masterclasses and much more, SACFF offers a wide range of programs for individuals from all levels looking to grow in the film and television industry in Canada.

Events

Our members get access to special industry and networking events that are organized to connect Canadian creators with each other.
